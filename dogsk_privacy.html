Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("the App") handles user information. Your privacy is important, and this App is designed to respect it.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require user accounts or registration
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or identifiers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not access contacts, photos, or location

2. Browsing Content

The App functions as a web browser. All websites you visit are loaded directly from their respective servers.

The App does not monitor, record, or log your browsing activity. Any data processing related to visited websites is governed by the privacy policies of those websites.

3. Local Device Storage

The App may store limited data locally on your device, such as:

  • Browser preferences (e.g., selected search engine)
  • Temporary cache or browsing state

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally. You can clear this data at any time by using the App's features or deleting the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party services for analytics, advertising, or user profiling.

External websites accessed through the App may collect data according to their own privacy policies. The App has no control over such data collection.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data, no such information can be stored.

6. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or store personal data, there is no risk of personal data leakage from the App itself.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App’s functionality changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.
